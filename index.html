about me
beyond the cv
Computer Science Information Technology University of KwaZulu-Natal Durban 2026

hi, i'm brae, an aspiring 21 year old computer scientist. i've always known tech would be my passion - from being featured on national television as a Nickelodeon Genius, my love for tech started when i was about 10 years old - i taught myself how to torrent TV shows, burn them onto DVDs and sell them to my friends and family for fun.

my philosophy towards development is that if software doesn't look good, people won't want to use it (myself included). i'm a bit of a perfectionist when it comes to design and extremely iterative when it comes to coding. i can't just leave things as they are if i know it can be improved.

as a current graphic designer, my dream is to create software applications people love to not just use, but enjoy interacting with.

while software development has been a major focus of my academic journey, it isn't my only passion. i'm particularly interested in fintech, and i'd love to work for a bank or investment firm one day. i enjoy sitting at the intersection of finance, tech and design, which is why i'm drawn to both software engineering and fintech.

i made this website to showcase everything that you can't see in a traditional CV. plus, it also gave me an excuse to make a fun holiday project, so i'm hoping you enjoy exploring it.
